About Us

How we Work

2016 is a year of new things. Of beginnings, new friendships, and new technology.

Keeping the spirit of innovation alive, we are proud to present to you Readefine, a Kolkata-based startup which brings you a revolutionary application as a service (aPaas), which will quite literally redefine the way you attain and read books.

Have you ever felt a burning desire to read a particular book, but given up hope because none of your friends have it? We are ardent readers and know how this feels. In the age of technology, however, there is a simple technical solution to everything. Presenting Readefine.

Once the app is installed, scroll to select your city, and then type in the details of the book you are looking for: Name, Author and Publisher. We'll do the rest. Our extensive database, collected over the last six months spans every title available in most prominent bookstores, neighborhood libraries and also owned by generous locals who are happy to sell or lend it to you.

The next step is to select any one of the listings provided, and we'll display their contact details. Call them up, meet them at any agreed-upon location, and voila! The book you've been looking for is now yours to read and cherish forever.
